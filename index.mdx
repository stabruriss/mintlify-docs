The Portal API is the developer surface of the Vibrant Wellness platform. Use it to integrate functional-medicine lab testing into your EHR, telehealth, or coaching product without standing up your own logistics, accessioning, or reporting stack.

## What the API can do today

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Orders" icon="clipboard-list" href="/concepts/orders">
    Create, list, update, and cancel lab test orders for any panel in your catalog.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tracking" icon="route" href="/guides/track-order">
    Follow an order from kit shipment through sample receipt to lab completion.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports" icon="chart-line" href="/concepts/reports">
    Fetch finalized reports as structured JSON or render-ready PDFs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Webhooks" icon="bell" href="/platform/webhooks">

  </Card>
